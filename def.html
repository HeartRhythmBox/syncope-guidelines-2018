Transient Loss of Consciousness (T-LOC)
  • TRANSIENT
    short duration; <5mins
  • UNRESPONSIVE
    no response to speech/touch/pain. required witness.
  • UNAWARE
    loss of memory.
  • UNCONTROLLED
    fall, abnormal movement.
Cardiac Arrest is not T-LOC.
